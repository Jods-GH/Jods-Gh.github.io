Dungeon Auras

Dungeon WeakAuras provide visual and auditory cues during dungeon encounters to enhance player awareness of mechanics. They create customizable alerts such as symbols, animations, and sounds that warn players about specific boss abilities or dangerous situations. These cues help players react quickly and make better decisions, ultimately improving their performance and contributing to more successful dungeon runs. You can find them directly on Wago here.

Miscellaneous

This collection offers you a wide range of WeakAuras to support you in all aspects of the game. From the Raid Ability Timeline that shows you important boss abilities in real-time, to the Interrupt Tracker that ensures you never waste a crucial interrupt effect. Additionally, you'll find auras that monitor your character statistics. Whether you're mastering raids, tackling Mythic+ dungeons, or simply looking to optimize your performance – these auras help you stay on top and get the most out of your character.

Raid Ability Timeline

The Raid Ability Timeline is a powerful WeakAura that displays important boss abilities in real-time. With this aura, you can precisely time and better react to the mechanics of a boss fight. It visualizes the abilities in a clear timeline, so you always know what’s coming next. Perfect for planning your cooldowns and ensuring you stay one step ahead.

Interrupt Tracker

The Mythic Interrupt Tracker is your ultimate support tool for ensuring you never miss a crucial interrupt in Mythic+ dungeons. This WeakAura not only shows which of your group members have already used an interrupt, but also what they have interrupted. This way, you always stay informed and ensure that critical spells are interrupted promptly.

CC Expiration tracker

This WeakAura shows you the remaining duration of soft CC effects (such as Hex, Polymorph, etc.) on your enemies. It helps you to recognize in advance when a CC effect is about to end, allowing you to better plan your next steps. Ideal for keeping track of important CCs and adjusting your reactions accordingly.

Character Stats

The Character Stats WeakAura provides you with a resource-efficient and precise overview of your character's most important statistics. This aura displays your key stats such as main stats, haste, versatility, and other relevant attributes at a glance, without consuming valuable resources. Ideal for players who want to stay informed without impacting game performance. This allows you to focus on what matters and ensures you're always optimally prepared for any challenge.
