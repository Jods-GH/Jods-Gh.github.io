Welcome to my Pokémon StreamAvatars-Game!

When you enter my stream and grant permission to StreamAvatars, you'll receive your own Pokémon avatar.
In the "About Me" section of the extension, you can choose your avatar.
Initially, you can only choose between the starter Pokémon from generations 1 to 6.

Catching and Evolving Pokemon

Every 15 minutes, a random Pokémon will appear in the stream, and you can try to catch it with the command "!catch [pokemonname]".
Each Pokémon is active for 300 seconds, followed by 900 seconds of inactivity before a new one spawns.
So, always keep an eye out and be ready to catch the next Pokémon!

When you have collected enough gold, you can evolve your avatar with the command "!evolve [pokemonname]".

  • 500 gold for the first evolution
  • 1000 gold for the second one

Events & Bossfights

Additionally, there are special events that you can purchase with channel points. Battle Royales and Boss fights will award you with additional Pokémon if you win.

The Boss fights have different rarity levels and drop the following:

  • Simple Bosses + Duel Victory: Common Pokémon.
  • Normal Bosses + Battle Royale Victory: Rare Pokémon.
  • Hard Bosses: Epic Pokémon.

Each boss has its own loot table with increased drop chances for specific types of Pokémon:

  • Mad King: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 1.
  • Scrollkeeper: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 2.
  • Ancient Grandmaster: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 3.
  • Eye: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 4.
  • Bidoof: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 5.
  • Gigantamax Charizard: Drops more Pokémon from Generation 6.
  • Pumpkin King: Drops Pokémon from all generations equally.

Effects

There are two different effects to unlock.

  • Hearts: for the viewer with the most !hugs.
  • Stars: for the viewer who has collected the most stars with !jump.

The leaders for each of these effects can be seen in the leaderboards in the extension.

Nametags

Depending on your role in the stream, you will receive different name tags:

  • Viewer
  • Follower
  • Subscriber
  • Mod
  • Jods

Have fun playing, catching your Pokémon, and interacting with the community!
